X-37B
X-37B is an experimental re-usable spaceplane, very similar to the Shuttle but much smaller and completely robotic and using more advanced technologies
  • The X-37B is 29 feet long and has a wingspan of just over 14 feet across.
  • X-37B uses its own solar array and lithium ion batteries to generate power, instead of Shuttle's fuel cells, a major reason why it can stay on orbit for much longer
  • X-37B has thrusters for on-orbit maneuvering and de-orbit, but no engines for powered flight in the air. It is a glider in the atmosphere.
