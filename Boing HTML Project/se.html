    • Senior Executive - Shane
    Shane Mckenna is a hardware engineer working at Boing. He was formerly Senior Vice President of Technologies, before leaving that role to focus on unnamed future products. It has been claimed that he supervises the development of Boings B2 or X37B. Mckenna earned a BSEE degree from the University of Limrick in 2009. He then went on to fill the positions of senior director at SGI and vice president of Engineering at Raycer Dynamics, subsequently acquired by Boing in 2012.
